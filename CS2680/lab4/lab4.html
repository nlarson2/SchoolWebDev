FAQ

Q: What is the CSS box model?

The box that makes up the total width and height of an elements. (padding, margin, content)

Q: What is the differene between margin and padding?

Margin is the distance from the edge of the box model to the border of the content and padding is the distance from the border to the content of the element.

Q: How is an HTML element height and width determined?

It is determined by the margins, padding, and size of the internal content.

Q: Explain this CSS property: padding:25px 50px;

This sets the padding to an element. Top and bottom padding will be 25px each and left and right padding will be 50px each.

Q:What is the difference between a border and an outline?

Border is the styling at the outer edge of the padding, and the ourline is the styling of the margin space.
